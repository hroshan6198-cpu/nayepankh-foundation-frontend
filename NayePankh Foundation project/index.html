About NayePankh Foundation

We believe every person deserves dignity, education, and support. Our mission is to bring positive change by connecting volunteers, donors, and communities through meaningful social work.

Our Mission

To help underprivileged communities with education, awareness, and basic support.

Our Vision

To build a society where every child and family gets equal opportunity to grow.

Our Values

Compassion, transparency, teamwork, and long-term social impact.
